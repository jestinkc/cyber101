# About the Program

*Cyber 101* is a two-day cybersecurity event designed to introduce students to the world of cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and digital protection.

The program focuses on building awareness about cyber threats, understanding ethical hacking concepts, and providing practical exposure to cybersecurity tools used by professionals.

Participants will gain both *theoretical knowledge and practical insights* from experienced industry experts.
